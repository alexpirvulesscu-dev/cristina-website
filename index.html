The method

Precision you can see
before you decide.

  1. Scan

    The whole face first

    No plan starts at a tooth. It starts with how your face moves, rests and smiles — because that is where dental work either belongs, or shows.

  2. Measure

    Proportions, not guesses

    Symmetry, lip line, the width of each tooth against the next — measured and mapped before anything is touched. You see the same data I do.

  3. Plan

    Tooth by tooth

    Each tooth is its own decision: keep, whiten, reshape, restore. The most conservative option that achieves the result always wins.

Her story

GlasgowLondonBucharest

I grew up in Craiova, and I left Romania to learn dentistry the way I wanted to practice it — slowly, precisely, with the patient in the room for every decision.

Glasgow taught me the discipline: the standards, the documentation, the habit of never touching a healthy tooth without a reason. London taught me the craft — years spent planning smiles for people whose faces are photographed for a living.

“I came home to Bucharest because the work I love doing best is quiet, careful — and it belongs here too.”

What I brought back is a way of working: UK-standard consent and planning, conservative by default, honest about what is and isn’t worth doing to your teeth.
