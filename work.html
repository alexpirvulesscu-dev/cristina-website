Case — Full rehabilitation

Ready for her wedding day

She wanted her dream smile before her wedding — without orthodontics and without implants. Gum health came first, then caries removal, eight weeks of whitening, the old crowns and bridges replaced, and the gum line contoured to an even smile.

  • Staged over months, finished in time
  • Old crowns & bridges replaced
  • No orthodontics, no implants
Case — Invisalign

Straightened, kept intact

She wanted an alternative to ceramics — her own teeth, not covered ones. Eighteen months of Invisalign, three weeks of home whitening, then small composite additions to soften two sharp edges. No drilling, nothing irreversible.

  • Invisalign Full — 18 months
  • Home whitening — 3 weeks
  • Composite reshaping — 2 teeth
Case — Veneers for peg laterals

Two small teeth, completed

She was born with two undersized "peg" lateral incisors. After whitening, the gum line around them was evened, then two minimal-prep porcelain veneers brought the teeth up to the size nature intended — and nothing else was touched.

  • Porcelain veneers — 2 teeth only
  • Gum contouring for even heights
  • Home whitening — 2 weeks
Case — Composite restoration

A filling you can’t find

An old metal filling, replaced with composite built up in layers to match the tooth’s natural anatomy — shade, translucency, even the fine grooves and staining of the neighbouring teeth. No crown, no lab, one visit.

  • Direct composite — 1 tooth
  • Layered to natural anatomy
  • No crown needed
Case — Single veneer

One veneer, zero drilling

A single non-prep porcelain veneer on one tooth, fitted without cutting into the healthy tooth underneath. If she ever chooses orthodontics later, her own tooth is fully intact beneath it — that reversibility was the point.

  • Non-prep porcelain veneer — 1 tooth
  • Home whitening — 2 weeks
  • Healthy tooth untouched beneath
Case — Complex rehabilitation

Rebuilt, in the right order

Worn teeth, old restorations, one missing tooth. The rebuild was staged over months: whitening first, then Emax crowns, an implant crown, and a night splint to protect the finished work while she sleeps.

  • Emax crowns + implant crown
  • Staged whitening before ceramics
  • Night splint for long-term protection
